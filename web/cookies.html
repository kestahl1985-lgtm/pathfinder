Cookie Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how this Vula website uses cookies and similar technologies.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites work and can collect basic usage information.

How we use cookies

Our website is intentionally lightweight. We currently use only what is necessary to display the site:

TypePurpose
EssentialNeeded for the website to load and function correctly.
Web fontsWe load fonts from Google Fonts to display the site; this may set cookies controlled by Google.

We do not currently use advertising or tracking cookies. The Vula assessment itself runs on WhatsApp, which does not use website cookies.

📌 If you later add analytics (for example Google Analytics or Meta Pixel), update this page and add a cookie-consent banner that lets visitors accept or decline non-essential cookies.

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect how the site works.

More information
