2. Purpose of this Manual

This manual is published in terms of section 51 of PAIA. It allows members of the public to:

  1. Identify the categories of records held by Vula Careers that are available without a formal PAIA request;
  2. Understand how to make a formal request for access to records;
  3. Know which records are available under other legislation;
  4. Obtain the contact details of the Information Officer;
  5. Understand how Vula Careers processes personal information and what security measures are in place.

4. Guide on How to Use PAIA

The Information Regulator has published a guide on how to use PAIA (the "Guide"), available in all official languages and in braille, which explains:

  1. The objects of PAIA and POPIA;
  2. The contact details of Information Officers of public and private bodies;
  3. The manner and form of making a request for access to records;
  4. The assistance available from the Information Regulator;
  5. Remedies available if a request is refused.

The Guide can be obtained:

  1. Upon request to our Information Officer at paia@vulacareers.co.za; or
  2. From the Information Regulator's website: www.justice.gov.za/inforeg/

A copy of the Guide is available for public inspection during normal business hours in English and isiZulu.

8. Processing of Personal Information

8.1 Purpose of Processing

Vula Careers processes personal information to:

  1. Deliver free RIASEC career assessments to South African high-school learners via WhatsApp;
  2. Generate personalised career guidance reports;
  3. Recommend relevant study pathways, institutions and bursary opportunities;
  4. Connect consenting learners with educational institutions and bursary providers; and
  5. Comply with legal obligations.

8.5 Information Security Measures

Vula Careers implements the following security measures to protect personal information:

  1. Encryption in transit: All connections use HTTPS/TLS;
  2. Database access controls: Row-Level Security restricts database access; anonymous access is disabled;
  3. Webhook authentication: Twilio webhook requests are validated by cryptographic signature before processing;
  4. Token-gated reports: Career report PDFs are accessible only via a unique secure token;
  5. Minimal data collection: Only information necessary for the service is collected;
  6. Deletion on request: Users may reply DELETE via WhatsApp to have their data removed; and
  7. Restricted admin access: Dashboard access is protected by authenticated login.
