Guidance, not professional advice

Vula provides general career-guidance information based on a self-reported interest assessment (a Holland/RIASEC-style questionnaire). It is intended to help learners explore options and start conversations about their future.

Vula is not a registered career counsellor, psychologist, or educational institution, and the Service does not constitute professional career, psychological, legal or financial advice.

Verify before you decide

  • School subject requirements, university and college admission criteria, qualification pathways, and bursary terms differ between institutions and change over time.
  • Always confirm current, official requirements directly with the relevant school, institution or funder before making decisions about subjects, applications or funding.
  • Where possible, also speak to your Life Orientation teacher, a qualified career counsellor, or your parent/guardian.

No guarantees

The career matches and information Vula provides are suggestions only. We do not guarantee admission, funding, qualification, employment or any specific outcome, and we are not responsible for decisions made based on the guidance.

Accuracy
