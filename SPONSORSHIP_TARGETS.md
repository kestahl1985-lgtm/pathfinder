# Vula Careers — Sponsorship Target List

**Important caveat before using this list**: the institutions and companies below are real, well-known South African organisations selected because their profile plausibly fits sponsoring Vula (they recruit the age/interest group Vula reaches, or have relevant CSI/Skills Development budgets). I have **not** verified current staff names, job titles, emails or phone numbers for any of them — those change often and guessing them would be actively misleading. For each target, this list tells you **which department to approach and why**; you'll need to find the actual current contact via the institution's website, LinkedIn, or a warm introduction before reaching out.

Prioritised for the **Cape Town-first launch** (per the current rollout plan), with national targets flagged for Phase 2+.

## How to prioritise within this list
1. Start with organisations that can decide and sign quickly (private colleges, smaller corporate CSI budgets) to get 2–3 **Founding Sponsors** live fast.
2. Run university/large-corporate outreach in parallel — their sales cycles are longer (procurement, committee sign-off), so start early even though they'll close later.
3. Use the first signed sponsor as a case study to accelerate every conversation after it.

## Suggested outreach sequence

1. **Week 1–2**: Cape Town private colleges + TVET colleges (fastest to close) — aim for 2–3 signed Founding Sponsors.
2. **Week 1–2 (parallel)**: Open conversations with UCT/Stellenbosch/UWC/CPUT recruitment offices and 3–4 corporate CSI contacts — expect longer lead times, so start the clock early.
3. **Week 3–6**: Use the first signed sponsor's early usage numbers as social proof in every subsequent pitch.
4. **Month 2–3**: Expand the same sequence to Johannesburg/Pretoria, then Durban, following the city-by-city rollout in MARKETING_PLAN.md.
