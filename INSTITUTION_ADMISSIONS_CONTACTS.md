# Vula Careers — Institution Admissions Contact List (Comprehensive)

**Last verified: 10 July 2026.** Compiled via live web research against each institution's own official site (or the DHET TVET college directory / other authoritative source as secondary). Every number below is sourced — where a source disagreed with another, or a number couldn't be independently confirmed, that's flagged explicitly (**NOTE —**) rather than picking one silently. **Do one verification call before a mass dial-out campaign**, especially for flagged entries — a meaningful number of official college/university websites blocked automated fetches or were temporarily down during this research, so several entries rest on search-engine corroboration or a secondary source rather than a direct page load.

This is the full national list: **26 public universities, 25 major private higher-education institutions, and all 50 public TVET colleges.** For the original Cape Town-first + Phase 2 sponsor prioritisation and corporate CSI/SETA/NGO contacts (not "admissions" lines), see SPONSORSHIP_TARGETS.md.

## Flags that affect outreach decisions, not just individual numbers

- **Damelin's registration is currently contested.** DHET issued a notice of intent to cancel Damelin's (and CityVarsity's and ICESA's) registration in Dec 2025 over unresolved compliance/financial-sustainability documentation; Damelin was appealing as of March 2026, and Educor (its parent) has bounced between deregistration and reinstatement multiple times since 2024. **CityVarsity and ICESA have since ceased operating entirely** (confirmed defunct, not included in this list). **Verify Damelin's current registration status before committing outreach effort there.** Sources: news24.com/southafrica/education (13 Dec 2025 notice); dailymaverick.co.za (11 Feb 2026, ongoing CCMA/compliance disputes).
- **Rosebank College → Rosebank International.** Advtech rebranded in May 2026; same institution, `rosebankcollege.co.za` now redirects to `rbi.ac.za`.
- **Varsity College + Vega + MSA + Monash → merged into "Emeris."** A 2026 merger of four Advtech-affiliated brands.
- **CTI / Pearson Institute of Higher Education** rebranded to **Eduvos** back in 2021 — not a separate institution, already covered under Eduvos below.
- **The Design School Southern Africa** has folded into Vega, itself now part of Emeris — not a separate listing.
