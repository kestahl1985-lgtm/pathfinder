## Reach the right students, before they've made up their mind.

**Vula** is a free, WhatsApp-native career-guidance tool for South African Grade 10–12 learners. In under 5 minutes, a learner answers 30 tappable questions and gets a personalised career profile, a downloadable PDF report, and — where relevant — sees your courses surfaced as the match for their interests and city.

## The problem you already know

Most SA schools have one career counsellor for hundreds of learners, if any at all. Learners lock in Grade 9/10 subject choices with almost no real information about what those choices open or close off. By the time your recruitment team reaches them in Grade 12, many have already ruled themselves out of your programmes without realising it.

## How it works for you

- A learner completes their profile on WhatsApp — no app, no login, works on any phone.
- If their RIASEC profile and city match your programme, your course is surfaced directly inside their results, at the exact moment they're exploring that career path.
- You're reaching prospective students by genuine interest fit and geography — not buying a generic list and hoping it lands.

## Why this is lower-risk than a data buy

Vula's model is sponsorship, not lead sales — deliberately. You get visibility with the right audience without becoming a second custodian of minors' personal information. That's a POPIA exposure most institutions would rather not take on, and Vula's structure means you never have to.
